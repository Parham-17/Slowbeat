# Slowbeat — App Icon Brief

A self-contained brief for any image generator (Midjourney, DALL·E, Imagen, Sora,
Adobe Firefly, etc.) to produce app-icon concepts. Copy any section into your prompt.

---

## The app, in one line

**Slowbeat — Pause. Breathe. Begin.**
A 60-second guided breath ritual that surfaces *before* important moments on your calendar.

## What the app actually does

Slowbeat watches your calendar for moments that matter — presentations, interviews,
hard conversations, performances, exams — and offers a single 60-second box-breathing
ritual right before each one.

The experience:
- A soft luminous orb expands and contracts with your breath (inhale fills, hold sustains, exhale releases, rest)
- Phase-textured haptics carry the rhythm on the wrist and in the palm
- After the breath, you write one if-then plan ("If X happens, I will Y") to carry into the moment

It is **not** a meditation library, a content app, a wellness tracker, or a sleep aid.
It is **one ritual, performed at one moment**.

## Emotional target

The user is two minutes away from something that matters. They are slightly nervous,
slightly braced. Slowbeat is the held breath of an athlete waiting for the starting gun —
**grounded, composed, ready**.

It is *not* sleepy, drowsy, dreamy, escapist, cuddly, or relaxing. It is *present*.

## Aesthetic principles

- **Soft, organic, dimensional** — layered light, gentle gradients, depth without busyness
- **Calm darkness** — most interaction happens against deep indigo / plum; the icon should sit comfortably on both a dark and light home screen
- **A single focal element** — one luminous thing in the centre, nothing else competing
- **Quiet authority** — this is a serious person's icon, not a cute self-care badge
- **Original** — no visual lineage with any existing breathing / meditation / mindfulness app

## Colour palette (hex)

These match the in-app design language. Use them, don't invent new ones.

**Backgrounds / void**
- `#0A0D24` deep night indigo
- `#1A1238` plum night
- `#2C1A4D` royal indigo

**Primary glow** (the luminous element)
- `#BFB3FA` soft lavender
- `#EAD2F5` rose-lilac highlight
- `#A8B3E6` periwinkle

**Optional warm tint** (sparing — at most a small accent, never dominant)
- `#F5B7B7` desaturated sunset rose

**Optional cool tint** (sparing)
- `#80CCC8` soft teal
- `#9EE0D6` mint

**Never use**: saturated red, orange, hot pink, neon anything, pure black.

## Visual reference points

Take inspiration from these — they are NOT apps:
- A **single pearl** suspended in dark velvet, lit softly from above
- An **aurora** seen through frosted glass
- A **jellyfish bell** — translucent layers, gently illuminated
- A **paper lantern** at dusk
- **Moonlight through cirrus cloud**
- **Bioluminescence** in deep water
- An **opal** with quiet inner fire
- A **droplet of mercury** with reflected light

## Hard avoid list

If the icon contains any of these, it has failed the brief:

- Human figures, silhouettes, faces, heads, brains, profiles
- Leaves, lotuses, flowers, mandalas, hands in mudras, butterflies
- Clocks, hourglasses, stopwatches, watch faces, dials
- Hearts, EKG lines, pulse waveforms, stethoscopes
- Petals folding open or closed
- Mountains, sunsets, landscapes, beaches, forests
- Cartoon mascots, characters, eyes, smiles
- App name as text inside the icon
- Sharp geometric logos (triangles, squares, perfect rings)
- Anything resembling a play button, refresh icon, or system glyph

## Hard avoid — competitors

The icon must not visually echo:
- Calm (water-droplet on landscape)
- Headspace (orange circle, friendly mascot)
- Breathwrk (cyan starburst)
- Othership (cosmic gradient with starburst)
- iBreathe (white circle on blue)
- Oak (illustrated leaf)
- Apple Mindfulness (folding flower petals)
- Insight Timer (singing bowl)
- Balance (white wave on dark)
- Lungy (anatomical lung shape)

## Technical specs

- Square, 1024 × 1024 px master
- PNG, no transparency (solid background)
- No rounded corners — iOS applies the corner mask system-wide
- No text overlays
- Must remain legible at 29 × 29 px (smallest size iOS renders)
- One bold focal element, one or two supporting layers — anything more disappears at small sizes

## Ready-to-paste prompt

> Minimal iOS app icon, square 1:1. A single soft luminous orb suspended in a deep
> indigo void. The orb is translucent and layered like a jellyfish bell or a pearl
> lit softly from above-left, with an inner glow of soft lavender (#BFB3FA) and
> rose-lilac (#EAD2F5) fading outward into deep night-indigo background
> (#0A0D24 to #1A1238). Subtle organic depth, multiple soft gradient layers,
> a barely-visible angular shimmer suggesting light catching the surface.
> Calm, present, grounded — not sleepy, not dreamy, not cute.
> No human figures, no leaves, no clocks, no hearts, no waveforms, no text,
> no mascots, no landscapes. Single focal element, readable at thumbnail size.
> Style: organic minimalism, soft volumetric gradient, not flat-design,
> not photo-realistic, not illustration.
